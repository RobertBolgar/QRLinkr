# BRAND_GUIDELINES.md

Version: 1.0

Status: Planning

Owner: Robert Bolgar

## Purpose

Define the QRLinkr brand identity, messaging, personality, and visual direction.

Every page, feature, and marketing asset should reinforce a consistent brand experience.

---

# Brand Position

QRLinkr is the premium free QR code generator.

It competes through simplicity, speed, trust, and quality—not feature overload.

---

# Mission

Help anyone create beautiful, professional QR codes in seconds.

No ads.

No watermark.

No account required.

---

# Vision

Become the most trusted QR code generator on the internet while naturally introducing users to TubeLinkr when they need analytics and attribution.

---

# Brand Personality

QRLinkr should feel:

- Professional
- Modern
- Fast
- Helpful
- Trustworthy
- Minimal

Avoid being:

- Loud
- Pushy
- Gimmicky
- Spammy
- Overly technical

---

# Brand Promise

Users should leave thinking:

"That was incredibly easy."

---

# Primary Tagline

Create beautiful QR codes for free.

---

# Supporting Messages

- No ads.
- No watermark.
- No account required.
- Generate in seconds.
- Built for everyone.

---

# Voice & Tone

Write with:

- Clarity
- Confidence
- Simplicity
- Honesty

Avoid marketing hype and exaggerated claims.

---

# Writing Style

Prefer:

Download PNG

Instead of:

Get Your FREE High Resolution QR Code Today!

Keep copy concise and useful.

---

# TubeLinkr Relationship

QRLinkr stands on its own as a complete product.

TubeLinkr is introduced only after users complete their primary task.

QRLinkr earns trust first.

TubeLinkr extends the experience.

---

# Logo Direction

The logo should be:

- Simple
- Flat
- Geometric
- Memorable
- Scalable

Avoid unnecessary detail.

---

# Color Direction

Use the Design System as the source of truth.

TubeLinkr Orange should be used as an accent—not the dominant interface color.

---

# Marketing Principles

Never use:

- Fake urgency
- Misleading messaging
- Dark patterns
- Forced signups

Trust is a competitive advantage.

---

# Success

QRLinkr succeeds when people recommend it because it is genuinely the easiest and most enjoyable QR code generator to use.
